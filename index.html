About us

"There is no other way to express yourself and feel yourself than dance...." So dance like no tomorrow.....

“I feel that the essence of dance is the expression of man – the landscape of his soul. I hope that every dance I do reveals something of myself or some wonderful thing a human can be.” Quotes Martha Graham.

Chilanka, Natyakalakshethram , is an eminent dance institution in Wayanad, approved by Kerala Sangeeth Nadaka Academy. We teach the language that expresses emotions through the body. A kind of emotional language that conveys the art of enjoyment to people through gestures, steps, song, and facial expressions.

When you dance, your purpose is not to get to a certain place on the floor. It's to enjoy each step along the way. We are inviting you to that enjoyment, entertainment and satisfaction ....
